About Me

Introduction

My name is Anna Tran and I received my BA in International Studies and minors in French and Rhetoric from Trinity College in 2023. I have experience in non-profits and community organizations, but underlying my work was my passion for storytelling.

I am interested in the intersection of visual communication and critical cultural awareness in different writing spaces. My long-term career goal is to work in the UX/UI or Technical Writing fields.
